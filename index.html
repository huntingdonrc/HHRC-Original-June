Houghton & Huntingdon Riding Club (HHRC) is a friendly club that caters for keen horse riders of all levels, who live in or around Huntingdon, a market town in Cambridgeshire, UK. It is based at the prestigious Houghton Hall Equestrian Centre and is part of Area 7 of the British Riding Clubs, and is affiliated the British Horse Society. HHRC teams compete in a number of dressage, show jumping, endurance and horse trials qualifiers throughout the year and we have lots of success making it through to championships and are frequently placed in the top rosettes. We pride ourselves on being an inclusive club and anyone that wants to be on a team will get a place on the team with no demands made on attendance at training sessions or other competition success. Whether you have a horse or pony or not, you can still become a member and have fun, as there is so much going on, such as...

  • Shows
  • Demonstrations
  • Junior and Senior Club Camps
  • Mounted and unmounted training days
  • Competitions
  • Social events

Arena Etiquette

Just a polite reminder. When visiting a venue for an event arranged by the Club, please remember to pick up your horse's droppings, especially from inside the arena. Spectators too are requested to help their team mates, and the Club, by ensuring droppings are collected. It is very important that you do this, as not doing so reflects badly on the whole Club and could lead to the Club being banned from using the venue in future. Thank you.

Important News - Championship Fees

In previous years the club has funded the entry fees for any member that has qualified to compete at a Championship.  However with the ever increasing costs of these events and also the fantastic number of our members that are qualifying we are no longer able to sustain this as a club.  We have kept our membership fees as low as possible and to make it fair to all members from 2016 competition year we will ask members to fund 50% of the championship fee.  This still represents a great return on investment for your £25 membership fee.  The committee hopes that you appreciate our position on this topic but if you have any concerns or questions then do direct them to our Chair on chairman@huntingdonrc.co.uk
