Training

All riders at training sessions must wear a medical armband or provide the trainer with emergency contact information prior to starting the session. It is the responsibility of the rider to ensure this information is provided. Thank you for your assistance with this.

The majority of our sessions are now available for online entry. This is our preferred method of entry as it keeps the administration to a minimum for our team of volunteers. In the event of cancellation of any session any person that has booked in will be notified by text, phone or email. If you have any questions on training then please email the club huntingdonrc@gmail.com. If you are not able to book in online then please download the training booking form below and send a cheque which must arrive at least 2 days prior to the training event. Do let us know if there is a particular trainer or discipline you would like us to use

About Our Trainers

Natalie Banks - Natalie has ridden internationally as a pony and junior rider, and has trained several of her own horses to advanced level, but she also has a great understanding of riders wanting to progress through the lower levels and gives positive and encouraging lessons. Peak Performance Horses

Jane Dawson-- Jane is a highly experienced rider who has taught RCS and PCS for many years. She believes in helping riders and horses of all abilities to improve. The majority of her work is dressage, show jumping and event training to affiliated level.

Tara Osborn-- Tara has ridden for the last 30 years and has vast experience with all types of equine. She has specialised in dressage for the last 15 years and has trained numerous horses and riders through to advanced level. Tara has produced a number of young horses from backing through their competitive career. Tara uses a sympathetic structured method of training for both horse and rider and trains each combination individually to suit their requirements. Long Drove Dressage - Tara

Beth Curtis-- Beth has international competition experience and has worked and trained with top professionals both in the UK and abroad. Beth is very experienced in identifying training needs in horses and riders and then working with them to enable rider and horses to achieve their potential whilst having a lot of fun along the way. BTC Equestrian

Fran Berry-- Fran runs regular sessions at Houghton Hall that are always oversubscribed. Fran is an II and has trained all levels for many years. She is extremely popular with all ages/abilities and still competes her own horses at BD dressage and Eventing.An immensely popular trainer

Anna Koch BHSPTT-- Anna runs regular sessions at Houghton Hall and has already run very successful and enjoyable sessions for HHRC this year.
