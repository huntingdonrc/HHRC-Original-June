Hunter Trial - 15th October 2017

Annual Hunter Trial at Home Farm, Alconbury on Sunday 15th October. This year this is also a qualifier for the Horseware National Hunter Trial Championships. Schedule will be available soon More information on the Horseware Championships can be found here Horsewear Ireland Facebook

Give it a Go Dressage

Give it a Go Dressage - running Thursdays 5pm to 9pm at Home Farm. 25th May, 8th June, 29th June and 13th July . Dressage tests will be geared towards those being used at other qualifiers and championships. We will also be running style jump training on 25th May, clear round on 8th June and show jump training on 29th June and 13th July. Taking online or postal entries - download the schedule here.
